Broccoli Salad

Description

Another easily packable, inexpensive, and fast meal.

I rely on this trustworthy recipe during my busy weeks, when I need to spend very little time on food but need nutrient-dense meals to power me through my work week.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound bacon
  • 2-3 large crowns of broccoli, chopped into florets
  • 1 red onion, diced
  • Dried cranberries
  • Sunflower seeds
  • Feta
  • Balsamic vinegar

Steps

  1. Cook up your bacon on the stovetop. When it cools, chop into chunks.
  2. In a large bowl, combine the broccoli, onion, and bacon chunks.
  3. Sprinkle with the diced cranberries and sunflower seeds.
  4. Serve it up and top with feta.
